Question: Determine which gates hide treasures and which traps.

Answer: Impossible to visit all 27 cubes in a cycle because the grid's coloring (black/white) prevents a Hamiltonian cycle. The robot can visit at most 26 cubes, forming a path but not returning to start.

2. The Paradoxical Gates

Answer: Gates B and C hide treasures; Gates A and D hide traps.
